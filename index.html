Firewatch Dashboard — Your Free Firewatch-Inspired Productivity Tool

Firewatch Dashboard is a stunning, free web application inspired by Firewatch, the award-winning first-person adventure game by Campo Santo. Featuring the iconic layered mountain sunset art style from Firewatch, this dashboard brings the serene wilderness aesthetic of the Shoshone National Forest directly to your browser.

Features of Firewatch Dashboard

  • Live Firewatch Clock — A beautiful real-time clock rendered against Firewatch-style sunset, evening, and night backgrounds with dynamic theme switching.
  • Firewatch Pomodoro Timer — Stay focused with a Pomodoro timer featuring preset configurations (25/5, 45/5, 50/10, 90/15), customizable durations, session tracking, and optional sound alerts.
  • Stopwatch — A precise stopwatch with lap functionality for time tracking.
  • Firewatch OST Player — Listen to the complete Firewatch Original Soundtrack by Chris Remo with playback controls, shuffle, repeat, volume control, and a full track list.
  • Multiple Themes — Switch between Firewatch sunset, Firewatch night, and Firewatch evening backgrounds, each faithfully recreating iconic scenes from the game.
  • Particle Effects — Ambient floating particles reminiscent of fireflies and dust motes in the Firewatch wilderness.

About Firewatch

Firewatch is a mystery adventure game developed by Campo Santo and published by Panic. Set in the Wyoming wilderness, players take on the role of a fire lookout named Henry stationed at a fire lookout tower in Shoshone National Forest during the summer of 1989. The game is renowned for its breathtaking art direction featuring layered mountain silhouettes, warm sunset gradients, and tranquil forest scenery — all of which inspired this Firewatch Dashboard.

Who Is This For?

Whether you're a fan of Firewatch looking for a Firewatch wallpaper alternative, a student seeking a beautiful Firewatch-themed Pomodoro timer, or someone who loves ambient aesthetic dashboards inspired by nature and wilderness, Firewatch Dashboard is the perfect free tool for your desktop or browser new tab page.
